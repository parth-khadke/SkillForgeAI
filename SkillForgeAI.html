Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from just watching YouTube tutorials?

YouTube tutorials are great, but they lack structure and validation. SkillForge AI gives you a clear roadmap, ensures you master fundamentals before advancing, and validates your learning through tests—no more tutorial hell where you watch endlessly without building real skills.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, 100% free. This is a college project built to solve a real problem in online education. No hidden fees, no credit card required, no premium tiers. We use free AI APIs (Google Gemini) to keep costs zero.

What skills can I learn?

Any skill you can describe! Programming (Python, JavaScript, Java), web development, data science, design, digital marketing, language learning—if you can name it, our AI can create a roadmap for it.

How long does it take to complete a skill?

It depends on your daily time commitment and the skill complexity. Most roadmaps range from 8-16 weeks. If you dedicate 1-2 hours daily, you can master most skills in about 12 weeks.

What if I fail a validation test?

No problem! You'll get detailed feedback on what you got wrong, targeted resources to review, and a retry test that's slightly easier after a 1-hour cooldown. The goal is to ensure you actually learn, not to gatekeep.

Can I learn multiple skills at once?

In the current version, you can have one active roadmap at a time to maintain focus. Once you complete it, you can start a new one. Future versions may support multiple concurrent roadmaps.
